Next steps:

To use this mobile app with your badges please setup a Mozilla account with a public group of badges. Please follow these steps:
  1. In your mobile web browser open http://www.openbadges.org and click the "get started" button.
  2. Collect the Badges 101 badge, create your account, and check your email to confirm it.;
  3. View your Mozilla Badge Backpack by following the link in the email or visiting http://www.openbadges.org and drag a badge into a public group (refresh the page if you can't see the 'public' checkbox). Making a group public may take 15 minutes.
Yes, I know that's a lot of steps. But it's worth it!

Then you will be ready to check or show your badges with the mobile app!
